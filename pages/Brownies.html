Party Brownies

These Party Brownies are a crowd-pleaser! Rich, fudgy brownies topped with a smooth chocolate frosting and a sprinkle of rainbow sprinkles. Perfect for any occasion!

These brownies were a hit at the party! The frosting sets perfectly, and the sprinkles add a fun touch. Definitely a recipe to keep in your dessert rotation!

These brownies are perfect for any gathering. The rich chocolate flavor combined with the colorful sprinkles makes them both delicious and festive. You’ll want to make these for every celebration!

Ingredients

  • 1 box brownie mix (usually 16–18 ounces)
  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon coarse kosher salt
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • Rainbow sprinkles

Instructions

  1. Make the brownies: Bake the brownies according to package directions in a 9×13 pan or slightly smaller rectangular pan.
  2. Make the frosting: Melt the butter in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add the milk, sugar, and vanilla; bring to a consistent boil over medium heat and cook for one minute.
  3. Mix in the chocolate: Add chocolate chips and stir to combine. If there are stubborn lumps, whisk for a minute.
  4. Frost the brownies: Pour the frosting over the brownies and spread evenly. Add sprinkles immediately as the frosting will start to set quickly.
  5. Rest and serve: Let the frosting rest for 15-30 minutes. Cut, serve, and enjoy the party!

These brownies are the perfect addition to any celebration. The frosting is rich and chocolatey, while the sprinkles add a fun, festive touch. Be sure to grab one before they’re all gone!
