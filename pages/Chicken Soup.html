Chicken Orzo Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons DeLallo Private Reserve Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced or minced
  • 1/4 cup DeLallo Tomato Paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup DeLallo Orzo Pasta
  • 2 cups chopped or shredded cooked chicken (pulled meat from a rotisserie chicken works well)
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1–2 cups water as desired
  • Parmesan, herbs, red pepper flakes, and lemon for serving
  • Bread for serving
  • Pesto for serving (optional – recipe in notes)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil: In a soup pot over medium-high heat. Add the garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until soft and fragrant but not browned.
  2. Add the tomato paste and seasoning: Cook until caramelized and turns a deep red color, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the broth: Gradually pour in the broth until the mixture becomes a smooth liquid. Season with salt to taste. Start with 1/2 teaspoon and adjust as needed.
  4. Cook the orzo: Add the orzo and bring to a simmer for about 10 minutes until the orzo is soft. Add more water or broth if needed to reach desired consistency. Stir in the heavy cream.
  5. Add chicken and lemon: Incorporate the chicken and squeeze in lemon juice to brighten the flavor.
  6. Serve: Top with Parmesan, herbs, red pepper flakes, and serve with hot crusty bread. Optional: Serve with a side of pesto for dipping the bread.

This Chicken Orzo Tomato Soup is a comforting and hearty meal perfect for any time of year. The rich flavors from the tomato paste and chicken combine beautifully with the orzo pasta, creating a deliciously satisfying soup.
